FaceWeather

FaceWeather is a simple android weather app designed especially for Chinese collge students.
We developed it with Android Studio.

Time

Nov 2015 - Jan 2016

Skills

UX research
Mobile UI design
Java

My roles

UX & UI desiger
Android developer

Inspiration

In China, almost every weather APP lacks of specificity. We suffer from information overload everyday. Thus, we want to build a simple but special weather app. As Chinese undergraduates, we decided to regard Chinese college students as our target audience.

How to make it a simple one?

Competitive analysis

In order to further our exploration of Chinese weather apps, we conducted competitive analysis of four popular weather apps

Needfinding

Interview

We then inverviewed 10 college students. We asked them about advantages and disadvantages of weather apps they are using, and their desirable weather apps. Then summarized their thoughts.

Discussion

After our investigation, we found that college students don't ask for much regarding weather apps. Their main concerns are:

  • rainy or sunny?
  • haze or clean?
  • what to wear today?

Hence, we decided to reduce the number of weather typies to only six.

Ideation

Enlightened by a Chinese buzzword "Kan lian",we planned to use six different faces to represent six different typies of weather correspondingly, which goal is to lighten users' burden of understanding the underlying meaning of each weather.

Design principles: simple, intuitive, convenient

Iterative design

Rapid prototyping & usability test

According to our ideation, I then made some wireframing mockup, proceeded to test and redesign. Inviting users to test prompted us to develop more efficiently. The purpose of usability test is to find problems with interaction design.

Visual design

Based on the usability test findings, I refined the wireframes. Also I discussed with other developers about our color palette. We then decided to use purple contrasting with pink as our theme color. All the visual design followed Google's Material Design principles.

Implementation

My role was to design and implemented layout of the app in Android Studio with XML. Also, I called online API to acquire weather information, parsed JSON data and then implemented interaction workflow with Java.
